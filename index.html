Thomas Fonseca


When I was 7 years old, I had my first contact of computer 4.86 with windows 3.11. I remember when I learned very fast just watching my uncles and my grandmother using it. Few days later I was using the computer without help. The time passed and my family had a hard time because my father lost his job, So I had to work early to help them. My passion for computers passed during this time because I could not have an opportunity to develop. When I was 18 years old, I earned a scholarship in Manager Production. That was not what I wanted but that was the only way to get a better job to help my family and save money to find what I love to do. Many years later working as a buyer in some companies and helping the IT department to bring solutions. The passion of technologic grew up again and I remember my first contact and how I felt. Now, 24 years later I have the opportunity to study and develop my skills.
